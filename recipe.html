Greek Yogurt Bowl

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Greek yogurt (plain or flavored)
  • 1/2 cup of mixed berries (such as strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1/4 cup of granola
  • 1 tablespoon of honey or maple syrup (optional, for sweetness)
  • 1 tablespoon of chia seeds or flaxseeds (optional, for extra nutrition)
  • Sliced almonds or other nuts (optional, for added crunch)

Method

Step 1
Start by spooning the Greek yogurt into a bowl. Arrange the mixed berries on top of the Greek yogurt, and sprinkle the granola over the berries and yogurt.
Step 2
Drizzle the honey or maple syrup over the granola for added sweetness. Optionally, sprinkle chia seeds or flaxseeds over the top for extra nutrition. If desired, garnish with sliced almonds or other nuts for added crunch.
Step 3
Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious and nutritious Greek yogurt bowl!

Mediterranean Chicken Meatballs

Ingredients

  • Ground chicken (you may also use round turkey, ground beef or ground pork)
  • Egg
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Sun dried tomatoes
  • Garlic (you may also use 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder)
  • Cheese (crumbled feta cheese and grated parmesan cheese are good options.)
  • Green onions
  • Fresh parsley (you may use other fresh herbs such as fresh mint, basil, fresh dill or oregano)
  • Salt + black pepper
  • Lemon (optional to enhance the flavor)

Method

Step 1
Mix the ingredients in a large bowl, combine ground chicken, egg, breadcrumbs, sun dried tomatoes, feta and parmesan cheese, garlic, green onions, parsley and season with salt and pepper. Mix the meatball mixture together using a spoon or your hands until all combined.
Step 2
Make the meatballs by using a small to medium cookie scoop. Make 2-inch sized balls and place on a parchment-lined baking sheet (or sprayed with non-stick spray). You should get about 18 meatballs.
Step 3
Pop the sheet pan in the oven at 400 degrees F and bake for about 20 to 25 minutes, until lightly browned and cooked through. Meatballs should have an internal temp of 165ºF when inserting a meat thermometer.
Step 4
Serve these Greek meatballs warm alongside a Mediterranean salad or make these bowls with brown rice, homemade tzatziki sauce (a cucumber greek yogurt-based sauce) and fresh veggies!

California Power Bowl

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked quinoa or brown rice
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup mixed salad greens
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup sliced cucumber
  • 1/4 cup sliced radishes
  • 1/4 cup sliced red onion
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Method

Step 1
Assemble the base by dividing the cooked quinoa or brown rice among serving bowls.
Step 2
Top the quinoa or rice with avocado slices, mixed salad greens, cherry tomatoes, shredded carrots, sliced cucumber, sliced radishes, and sliced red onion.
Step 3
In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper to make the dressing. Drizzle the dressing over the assembled power bowls.
Step 4
Serve immediately and enjoy your simple and nutritious California Power Bowl! Feel free to customize it with additional protein sources like grilled chicken or tofu if desired.
